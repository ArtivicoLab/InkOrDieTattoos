Female-Owned & Proud

Empowering Art, Retro Vibes

Welcome to Ink Or Die Tattoos, where retro meets modern in the heart of Decatur! As a female-owned studio, we're passionate about creating a space where everyone feels empowered to express their unique story through bold, beautiful ink.

Our talented team specializes in vibrant color work, classic black and gray pieces, and custom designs that celebrate individuality. We believe tattoos should be as unique as you are, and we're here to bring your vision to life with a fresh, retro-inspired twist.

From our pink-hued walls to our Instagram-worthy aesthetic, everything about Ink Or Die is designed to make you feel confident, comfortable, and ready to rock some seriously stunning ink!
